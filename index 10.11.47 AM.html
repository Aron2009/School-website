Christ the King School

Christ the King Catholic is proud to be a Catholic Community of Caring School. Our school strives to promote, model, and teach six key values. These values are Faith, Family, Caring, Trust, Respect, and Responsibility. All activities, interactions, and planning begins with these values at the core. We believe that all students will benefit from practicing these values when it comes to decisions both now and in the future. The Catholic Community of Caring philosophy is exciting because it permeates every aspect of our school. The six core values are incorporated into classroom learning, extra-curricular activities, school awards, playground activities, and our Catholic celebrations.

Prayer of the Month

Mother, help our faith! Open our ears to hear God’s word and to recognize His voice and call. Awaken in us a desire to follow in His footsteps, to go forth from our own land and to receive His promise. Help us to be touched by His love, that we may touch Him in faith. Help us to entrust ourselves fully to Him and to believe in his love, especially at times of trial, beneath the shadow of the cross, when our faith is called to mature. Sow in our faith the joy of the Risen One. Remind us that those who believe are never alone. Teach us to see all things with the eyes of Jesus, that He may be light for our path. And may this light of faith always increase in us, until the dawn of that undying day which is Christ Himself, your Son, our Lord!

  • 25th Annual Artwalk

    Every year, student artwork from each school is chosen to be displayed in our Central Office. Even though we are unable to celebrate our student artists together this year, we will still acknowledge and recognize all their wonderful creations! Here are three ways to enjoy Artwalk. Click or scroll through the book to experience and see a detailed image of all the pieces-index by school at the back.

  • Parent and Trustee Forum on March 30

    Our next online Parent & Trustee Forum will be held Tuesday, March 30, 2021 from 7-9 p.m. During the forum there will be: -Updates from CCSD's Board of Trustees -Opportunities for questions -A presentation from guest speaker Martin Parnell, an author, speaker and Guinness World Record holder for running 250 marathons in a year. Parnell has fundraised to help tens of thousands of underprivileged kids around the world and more than $1.5 million for the Right to Play organization. Most recently, he has been involved empowering women and girls in Afghanistan through sport and has launched the 3K Secret Run to fight for women’s rights and gender equality worldwide. Martin will bring us messages of resilience, equality and empowerment to parents through his personal story, so that they can share these valuable learnings with their children and families. All parents and school council chairs are invited to join. For Zoom meeting details, see our calendar event.

Educating And Empowering Students

We educate and empower students as we Live & Learn in our Faith. As Catholics, faith is a foundation of everything we do. Every school has an established Catholic Community of Caring that is based on the values of faith, care, respect, responsibility, trust and family. We work together to develop students who reflect the image of God and are successful contributors to our global society.
